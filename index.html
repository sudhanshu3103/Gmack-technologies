About Us

Innovative Formulations, Trusted Expertise Since 2003

Founded in 2003 by George Mackenzie, a seasoned expert with a BSc in Chemistry and a BBA, GMACK Technologies is a full-service consulting firm specializing in formulations for cosmetics, OTC, and HI&I. With over 25 years of experience in formulation and cGMP compliance, we are committed to innovation, quality, and regulatory excellence. Our expertise spans formulations, testing, quality control, production, training, regulatory affairs, and excess raw materials, ensuring our clients receive cutting-edge solutions tailored to their needs. At GMACK Technologies, we transform ideas into market-ready, high-performance products with precision and efficiency.
